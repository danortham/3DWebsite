About Me

Hello! My name is Daniel Northam and I am a nerd. I love playing video games, Dungeons and Dragons, skating, and juggling.

I recently earned my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and picked up a minor in Game Design and Development. Not only do I love playing games I have a passion for making games too.

I married the love of my life (Maliyah) on Spetember 20th 2020. I hope to be a full time software developer and make my own games in my free time.
